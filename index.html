Our Aim

Excel comes around each year, but some parts of it stay active throughout the year. One of those parts is the Social Initiatives. Through Excel, a lot of socially relevant initiatives are taken up and implemented, keeping in mind the need to give back to the society. Project Urja was one such initiative which was aimed to provide the village of Uriyampetti with electricity. Other than these, protection and restoration of nature has been given great importance as well. Be it cleaning of roads and beaches, or spreading awareness regarding the current condition of the environment, events are conducted each year under the branch of Social Initiatives to create a better world.
